# Business Model Canvas - loanflow-engine

## Value Propositions
- Cloud-native banking engine providing end-to-end loan origination and processing capabilities
- 60% reduction in operational costs through automation of manual lending workflows
- Real-time decision engine with AI-powered credit scoring for faster approvals
- Scalable infrastructure handling from startup to enterprise-level transaction volumes
- Built-in compliance frameworks for major financial regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- API-first architecture enabling seamless integration with existing fintech ecosystems

## Customer Segments
- **Primary**: Digital-first fintech companies (lending platforms, neobanks)
- **Secondary**: Traditional banks modernizing their digital lending capabilities
- **Tertiary**: Marketplace lenders and embedded finance providers
- **Geographic**: North America, Europe, and APAC markets with developed fintech ecosystems
- **Size**: Series B+ fintech companies with $10M+ in annual revenue

## Channels
- **Direct**: Cloud-based SaaS platform with self-service onboarding
- **Indirect**: Fintech incubators and accelerator partnerships
- **Sales**: Enterprise account executives for custom deployments
- **Digital**: Content marketing, webinars, and industry conference presence
- **Integration**: Third-party marketplace listings (AWS Marketplace, Azure Marketplace)

## Revenue Streams
- **Tiered SaaS subscriptions**: 
  - Basic: $2,500/month (up to 1,000 loans/month)
  - Professional: $7,500/month (up to 10,000 loans/month)
  - Enterprise: Custom pricing (10,000+ loans/month)
- **Transaction fees**: $0.50 per processed loan application
- **Implementation services**: One-time fee for custom integrations ($15,000-50,000)
- **Premium support**: 24/7 dedicated support at 15% of subscription fee
- **API call pricing**: $0.01 per API call beyond included monthly allowance

## Cost Structure
- **Technology**: Cloud infrastructure (35%), R&D (25%)
- **Sales & Marketing**: Customer acquisition (20%), content creation (10%)
- **Operations**: Customer support (15%), compliance monitoring (10%)
- **Personnel**: Engineering team (40%), product management (15%), sales (20%), support (15%), executive (10%)

## Key Resources
- **Technology**: Core loan processing engine, AI credit scoring models, compliance frameworks
- **Intellectual Property**: Proprietary risk assessment algorithms, workflow automation systems
- **Human Capital**: Fintech domain experts, compliance specialists, AI/ML engineers
- **Data**: Anonymized lending performance data, market benchmarks, regulatory updates
- **Infrastructure**: Cloud-native deployment architecture, security protocols, disaster recovery systems

## Key Activities
- **Product Development**: Continuous improvement of loan processing capabilities
- **Compliance Management**: Regular updates to regulatory frameworks across markets
- **Customer Success**: Implementation support, training, and optimization services
- **Risk Management**: Continuous monitoring of lending algorithms and performance metrics
- **Market Expansion**: Entry into new geographic markets with regulatory compliance
- **Partnership Development**: Integration with complementary fintech services

## Key Partners
- **Cloud Providers**: AWS, Google Cloud, Azure for infrastructure hosting
- **Financial Institutions**: Partner banks for funding and regulatory compliance
- **Regulatory Technology Providers**: Services for compliance monitoring and reporting
- **Fintech Ecosystems**: Integration platforms like Plaid, Stripe, and Dwolla
- **Data Providers**: Credit bureaus and alternative data sources for risk assessment
- **Industry Associations**: Fintech trade groups for market intelligence and advocacy
