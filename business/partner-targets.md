# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for Loanflow Engine

### Target Integrations

| SaaS/API                    | Free Tier Limits                      | Integration Effort | Value-Add                                         | Rationale                                                                 |
|-----------------------------|---------------------------------------|--------------------|--------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Stripe**                  | $0 for up to $1 million in revenue   | M                  | Payment processing for loan disbursement         | Facilitates seamless payment transactions, essential for fintech lenders. |
| **Plaid**                   | Free for up to 100 transactions/month | M                  | Bank account linking for user verification       | Enables lenders to verify borrower bank accounts, reducing fraud risk.   |
| **Twilio**                  | $15 credit for new users              | S                  | SMS notifications for loan updates               | Enhances user engagement through real-time communication.                 |
| **Zapier**                  | 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month                | S                  | Workflow automation for loan management          | Streamlines operations by connecting Loanflow with other SaaS tools.     |
| **Salesforce**              | Free trial available                   | L                  | CRM integration for lead management              | Supports customer relationship management, crucial for lenders.           |
| **Xero**                    | 30-day free trial                     | M                  | Accounting integration for financial tracking    | Helps lenders manage their financials efficiently.                        |
| **QuickBooks**              | 30-day free trial                     | M                  | Accounting integration for financial tracking    | Similar to Xero, provides essential financial management capabilities.    |
| **HubSpot**                 | Free tier available                   | M                  | Marketing automation for lead generation         | Supports customer acquisition strategies, vital for fintech growth.       |

### Prioritization Rationale
1. **Stripe** and **Plaid** are prioritized due to their direct relevance to the core functionalities of loan management and processing, enabling essential financial transactions and user verification.
2. **Twilio** and **Zapier** are included for their ability to enhance user engagement and automate workflows, which are critical for improving operational efficiency.
3. **Salesforce**, **Xero**, and **QuickBooks** are valuable for their CRM and accounting capabilities, providing comprehensive support for managing customer relationships and financial tracking.
4. **HubSpot** is included for its marketing automation features, which can help drive user acquisition and engagement in the competitive fintech landscape.

### Conclusion
Integrating with these SaaS/APIs will not only enhance the functionality of the Loanflow Engine but also provide significant value to fintech lenders by streamlining operations, improving user experience, and ensuring compliance with financial regulations. Prioritizing partnerships with revenue-share opportunities will also create additional revenue streams for the Loanflow Engine.
