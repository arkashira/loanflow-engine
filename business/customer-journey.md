# Customer Journey Map for LoanFlow Engine

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Fintech lenders experience inefficiencies in loan management and processing.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about available solutions; confusion over technical jargon; existing solutions are perceived as too complex or costly.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, curiosity.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (webinars, whitepapers) that demystifies loan management solutions; clear, jargon-free messaging.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with educational content.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Lenders actively search for loan management solutions to improve efficiency.
- **Friction Points**: Difficulty comparing features and pricing of various solutions; concerns about integration with existing systems; uncertainty about ROI.
- **User Emotions**: Overwhelmed, cautious, hopeful.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Interactive comparison tools; case studies showcasing successful implementations; transparent pricing models.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and inquiries.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Lenders sign up for a free trial or demo of LoanFlow Engine.
- **Friction Points**: Complicated onboarding process; lack of support during the trial period; limited functionality in the trial version.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement, skepticism, frustration.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Streamlined onboarding experience; dedicated support during the trial; access to full features for a limited time.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user engagement during the trial.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Lenders decide to purchase and implement LoanFlow Engine.
- **Friction Points**: Integration challenges with existing systems; resistance to change from staff; concerns about ongoing support.
- **User Emotions**: Relief, anxiety, anticipation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Comprehensive training programs for staff; seamless integration support; proactive customer service outreach.
- **Metric**: Time to full implementation and user satisfaction scores.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Lenders seek to scale operations and explore additional features or services.
- **Friction Points**: Limited awareness of additional features; concerns about costs associated with scaling; potential vendor lock-in.
- **User Emotions**: Optimism, uncertainty, eagerness.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized recommendations for scaling; loyalty programs or discounts for existing customers; regular updates on new features and enhancements.
- **Metric**: Customer lifetime value and upsell/cross-sell rates.
